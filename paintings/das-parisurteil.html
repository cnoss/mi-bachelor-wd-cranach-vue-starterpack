Three naked ladies stands in the middle (the goddesses Venus, Juno and Minerva), on the right side a horse standing behind a tree, on the left side a man in reddish clothes bent over a man in armor lying in the grass (the son of King Priam of Troy, Paris). The background of the painting is a landscape with mountains and trees. [Statens Museum for Kunst, revised 2013]
