This sensitive portrait shows a well-dressed young man seemingly transfixed by a devotional image - perhaps of the Virgin and Child - to which he offers a silent prayer as he pauses on a bead of the rosary held in his right hand. With his left hand, he clutches the fur border of his overcoat. On the reverse, a trompe-l"oeil image of a statue of a bald, bearded, and barefoot saint perhaps represents Saint Peter, but the work is too damaged for a definitive identification. [Cat. New York 2013, 45, No. 8]
